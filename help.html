📘 Help & Support

🚀 Getting Started

1. Sign up or log in.
2. Navigate to alarm, reminder, or calendar pages.
3. Use the bottom menu or side links to switch features.

📱 Using on Mobile

This app is mobile-friendly. For best performance:

  • Add to Home Screen for quick access
  • Allow sound & notification permissions
  • Keep the app open in background for alarms/reminders

⏰ Alarm Help

  • Use 24-hour format (e.g., 07:00, 18:30)
  • Add a label to identify the alarm
  • Use repeat for recurring alarms (daily, weekdays, etc.)
  • Alarms work in background if page is left open

🔔 Reminder Help

  • Set reminder message + exact time
  • Notifications & sounds will trigger at the exact minute

📅 Calendar Help

  • Shows full month view with holidays & Indian festivals
  • Sundays are marked red
  • Click on dates to see festivals/events

🔐 Login & Account

  • Use your email + password to log in
  • If you log out, you must log back in to access alarms/reminders

💡 Tips & Best Practices

  • Test alarms once after setting to make sure sound works
  • Use labels like "Wake Up", "Meeting", etc. for clarity

🧭 About This App

This task app helps you manage alarms, reminders, calendar events, and more — all in one place.

🛠 Features

  • Alarm: Set, edit, repeat alarms with custom sounds.
  • Reminder: Schedule reminders with notification and sound.
  • Calendar: View days, dates, festivals, and holidays.
  • Login/Signup: Secure your data using Firebase.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't my alarm ringing?

Ensure you gave permission for sound and background tasks. Keep the app open in the background.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored securely in your device and in Firebase if logged in.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. Most features like alarm and reminder work offline. Sync requires internet.

⚠️ Troubleshooting

I don't hear alarm sounds

Make sure volume is up and sound is enabled in your browser/app settings.

Reminders don't show notifications

Allow notifications in browser, and don't close the app tab completely.

App isn't saving alarms or reminders

Make sure you're logged in, and localStorage or Firebase is active. Check console for errors.
